Fun fact

Did you know that despite the popular belief that astronauts have to be highly physically fit,
NASA has actually sent a few astronauts to space who were not 100% healthy? For example, in 1998, John Glenn returned to space at the age of 77, making him the oldest person to ever fly in space. And in 2007, astronaut Clayton Anderson flew to the International Space Station despite suffering from mild hearing loss. NASA has said that it carefully evaluates the health of all astronauts before sending them to space, but these examples show that perfect health is not always a strict requirement
